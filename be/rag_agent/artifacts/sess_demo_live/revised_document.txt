**Section 1: Annual Reporting Obligations**
The Company shall submit a comprehensive annual report to the Investor within forty-five (45) days of the close of each fiscal year. This report must include audited greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions data, specific progress updates regarding all climate-related milestones, and a detailed disclosure of resource allocation. All submitted data must be verified by an independent third-party auditor. Failure to provide this report within the specified timeframe, or failure to meet the required data standards, shall be deemed a material breach of this Agreement.
